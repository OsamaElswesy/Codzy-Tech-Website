Our Mission

To bridge the gap between academic education and the real-world tech industry by providing mentorship-driven, project-based learning for the next generation.

Why We Started

Codzy Tech was founded by a group of senior software engineers who noticed a critical disconnect: students were graduating with degrees but without the practical skills needed to build real software.

We decided to change that. By focusing on "learning by doing," we've created a curriculum that mirrors actual software development environments. For kids, we focus on logic and creativity. For professionals, we focus on architecture, scalability, and deployment.
